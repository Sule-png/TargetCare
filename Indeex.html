Target Care Logistics

At Target Care, we are redefining logistics in Nigeria and beyond. As a proudly Nigerian-led cargo and logistics brand, we believe in connecting people, businesses, and markets through seamless shipping, digital logistics, and end-to-end freight solutions.

Whether you’re moving goods by air, sea, or land — locally or globally — Target Care provides scalable logistics services with precision and professionalism. Our mission is simple: to empower commerce, simplify movement, and deliver peace of mind to our customers, every step of the way.

With a growing footprint and a modern approach to supply chain management, we serve individuals, small businesses, and multinational corporations. Our commitment to speed, safety, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the logistics industry.

Our Core Services

  • Supply Chain Management: We design, monitor, and optimize the movement of goods from origin to destination.

  • Global Freight: We move cargo across continents via a trusted network of international partners and carriers.
  • Air Freight: Urgent and high-value goods? We deliver swiftly using our air freight channels.

Our Services

At Target Care, we are more than just a logistics company — we are your strategic partner in efficient global transport, warehousing, and end-to-end delivery. Our services are tailored to meet the diverse and complex needs of today’s fast-moving world. Whether you’re shipping across cities, continents, or oceans, Target Care ensures your freight moves swiftly, securely, and reliably.

1. Supply Chain Management

Our supply chain services are designed to give you full control and transparency across your logistics network. From procurement to delivery, we help businesses manage every stage of their supply chain through intelligent planning, tracking, and optimization. We leverage technology, analytics, and decades of expertise to streamline operations and reduce costs.

Whether you run a retail operation, an e-commerce store, or a manufacturing business, Target Care integrates seamlessly with your systems to provide flexible, real-time supply chain solutions. We reduce inefficiencies, minimize delays, and help you respond to market changes quickly. The result? A resilient supply chain that’s built to adapt and thrive.

2. Warehousing Solutions

Our warehousing services go beyond simple storage. We offer secure, climate-controlled, strategically located facilities that ensure your goods are protected and easy to access at all times. Whether you need long-term inventory storage or short-term cross-docking, our warehousing infrastructure is scalable and flexible.

Additionally, we provide value-added services like inventory management, order fulfillment, packaging, labeling, and distribution. Our smart warehousing system allows you to monitor your stock levels in real time, forecast needs, and improve overall efficiency. By partnering with us, you gain peace of mind and powerful logistical support under one roof.

3. Global Freight Services

With a network that spans across continents, Target Care offers comprehensive global freight forwarding solutions. We manage the complexity of international shipping, so you don’t have to. From customs clearance to regulatory compliance, we handle every detail to ensure smooth and timely delivery of your goods.

Our global freight services are ideal for businesses looking to expand their reach or optimize cross-border supply chains. With advanced tracking systems and dedicated support teams, we keep you informed and in control of every shipment, no matter where in the world it’s going.

4. Air Freight

When speed is critical, air cargo is the solution — and Target Care delivers. Our air freight services are fast, secure, and globally connected. We partner with major airlines and operate through international airports to get your goods where they need to be in the shortest time possible.

Ideal for perishable items, urgent deliveries, or high-value shipments, our air freight offering includes door-to-door service, customs handling, and insurance options. We also provide shipment consolidation to help reduce costs without sacrificing speed. Globally Target care keeps your air shipments flying smoothly.

5. Sea Cargo

Sea cargo is the backbone of global trade, and Target Care brings you reliable and cost-effective ocean freight solutions. We offer both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) options depending on your cargo volume and budget. From bulky machinery to consumer goods, we move it all.

With trusted partnerships at major seaports and years of experience navigating international trade regulations, Target Care ensures that your sea freight is handled professionally at every step. Our services include container tracking, insurance, documentation, and customs clearance. You can rely on us to deliver ocean freight that’s economical, secure, and on time.

6. Door-to-Door Delivery

For maximum convenience and peace of mind, our door-to-door delivery service handles every detail — from pickup at the origin to final drop-off at the destination. Whether you’re shipping to a residential customer or a business client, we make the experience simple and worry-free.

This service is available for both local and international deliveries. We pick up your goods, transport them via air, land, or sea, and deliver them directly to your recipient’s doorstep. With transparent tracking and 24/7 support, you stay informed every step of the way. Target Care makes shipping easier, more efficient, and more personal.
